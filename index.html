Associate Research Engineer, SWM, South Korea

I am an Associate Research Engineer at SW Mobility (SWM), specializing in the design and development of computationally efficient and robust trajectory prediction and motion planning frameworks for Autonomous Vehicles. My work focuses on advancing AI for Autonomous Driving, with expertise in trajectory prediction, multi-agent interaction modeling, and decision-making under uncertainty. Passionate about advancing research in Intelligent Autonomous Systems.
